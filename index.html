Quick Use Guide

  1. Use the left toolbar to choose a tool, then click in the viewport to add or work on metaballs.
  2. Select a ball to adjust its size, strength, color, or material-related appearance controls.
  3. Use the right sidebar for custom presets, export options, and transform helpers.
  4. Save Project stores the editable StickyMesh © scene. Load Project restores it later for continued sculpting.
  5. Convert to Mesh bakes the current metaball result. Export GLB exports the current result as a .glb file.
  6. Undo and redo preserve editing history across the sculpt workflow, including major transform and project-state actions.
